 # About iLEAP

 iLEAP makes it possible to measure, verify, and continuously improve the CO2e emissions of every shipment.

## Our Mission

 To deliver open standards[^1] and digital infrastructure[^2][^3] for measuring, reporting, and reducing logistics emissions globally. iLEAP enables the exchange of trusted emissions data based on the GLEC Framework and ISO 14083. It covers all geographies, transport modes and relevant business scenarios. Nurturing and scaling a strong community of implementers and supporters is a central element of iLEAP's success.
